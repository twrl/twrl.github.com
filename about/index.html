Tom Robbins

I’m Tom, and this is my blog/online dumping ground. Hello.

I’m a mathematics student at the University of Reading, who used to be a web developer (and before that I was several other things). I mostly write about computers and programming here - after all this is Github. Occasionally I take a detour into some social issue on which I have views.

I like parentheticals of all flavours.

I rarely code in public any more, partly because I’m a bit rusty and it can get a bit embarrassing, but mostly because I’m more inclined to just tinker with things and write about it. I’m reassured by the number of computer science students I know who ask me for help with their programming modules.
